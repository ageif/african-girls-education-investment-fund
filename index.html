Securing the future of African girls

The African Girls Education Investment Fund aims to secure the education of underprivileged girls in Africa by leveraging on the power of finance and investments.

Find Out More

Give once, impact forever.

At the African Girls Education Investment Fund, we believe the causes as important as the education of the African girl child should not be entirely dependent on the often unstable flow of donations from altruistic individuals.


As such we have designed a model that takes the donations that are already available to us and put them to work to generate more funds to support our girls. By doing this, we enable a donor to have a continued impact on the lives of the girls that we support, even if that donor just donated once.

How we work

We accept contributions

We accept contributions from people who want to support our good cause. These contributions enlarge the principal amount of our investments.

We perform research on safe financial assets to invest in

We then perform a relentless search to identify safe global financial assets that we can invest in.

We invest globally

We invest in financial assets located in different parts of the world to help diversify our investment portfolio.

We use profits to support our girls

We then use the proceeds from our investments to invest in the education of the girls that we support. We do this by paying for first-class tuition, buying them educational resources such as books and paying for any beneficial educational experience that they may benefit from such as math and science camps.
